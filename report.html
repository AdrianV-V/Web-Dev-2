The journey of acquiring fundamental skills in web development has been both exhilarating and demanding. Throughout the term, I delved into HTML and CSS engaging with coding, design principles, and the debugging process. In summary, this experience has provided me with a solid foundation in web development. Despite encountering various challenges, the fulfilment derived from creating functional and aesthetically pleasing projects made the effort worthwhile. I am eager to further develop my skills and tackle more complex projects in the future.
